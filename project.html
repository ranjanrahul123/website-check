WELCOME TO OUR PROJECT

Admission to Educational Institution

Our project is based on admission of students to educational institution based on their previous educational qualification. This will be handy for the students those who doesn't know which institution would be apt for them.
We will be having two tables Students and Institutions. Students table includes full name,date of birth,marks obtained in 12th class as it's attributes. Institution table includes institution name,institution location and cut off marks as it's attributes.
For each students, list of institution will be presented if they met the respective Institutions cutoff marks.
