CGPA ANALYZER


Our project name is CGPA Assistant. The user has to enter his current CGPA and the marks of the completed quizes in the current semester and he can find what his GPA and CGPA after this semester. The user can also set a target GPA ,and he will find how much exactly he should score in the upcoming quizes and end sem exams to achieve that target.
